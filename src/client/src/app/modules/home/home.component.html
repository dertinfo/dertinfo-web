About Us

dertinfo.co.uk is a website that intends to help in the running of the annual Dancing England Rapper Tournament for those organisers who take on the challenge. It supports them through the processes of registration, collecting scores and publishing results to teams and the public. It is updated and maintained by David Hall (a member of Black Swan Rapper).
