How To Judge

Right Then... So we've got some entries and those of you who are our official judges have been contacted. We’re just doing a bit of sorting out and we’ll be ready to go.

Official Judges

Shortly you’ll be receiving an email that will give you a password that will allow you to get into the judging section of the site. Please don’t share this with anyone else.

You’ll give your scores and comments and then we’ll keep hold of them until Saturday the 27th of March.

You don’t have to do it all at once. Just make sure you’re done before the 27th. If you decide to spread it over a couple of days just be aware that we get rid of your identity when you close the browser. You can pick up where you left off for up to 6 days but after those 6 days “computer says no!”.

Sometime on the Saturday we’ll forward the score sheets to the teams. The teams will be able to review what scores you gave them and make a guesstimate as to whether they’ve done enough to win. Towards the end of the day we’ll publish the collated results table to see who officially won.


Everyone Else

In the morning of the 27th we’re going to remove the block for everyone to get in and view the submissions to give their scores and comments.

You do not have to judge every dance but for each dance you score then we’ll take all the scores from everyone for that dance and then divide by the number that we have. (I could have just used the word average)

When you submit your “score sheet” we’ll be immediately forwarding it to the teams. So they can see what scores they are getting from you guys as they come in.

However before you start considering sabotaging teams by giving them really low scores or promoting your own them with really high scores we’ve also given the teams the ability to complain. (And we’ll be watching for the outlier high ones - as noone is going to complain at all 15’s) So when they get your score card then they can let us know if they are not happy.

In order to be completely transparent if we get complaints then we’ve got the control to wipe out a single result or wipe out the results from a whole person. To be clear we will do this without justification or notification to you. We want everyone to have fun so please keep that in mind.
