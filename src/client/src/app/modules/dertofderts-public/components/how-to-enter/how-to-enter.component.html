How To Enter

Hey you, thanks for coming to see what's going on with DERT of DERTs. We're kinda excited about seeing what you guys put forward for everyone to have a look at and also interested in seeing what you guys think of other dances from the past.

#1
 Read the rules

We're going to be asking you to send videos so the rules for video entries are:

  • Your video must be pre-recorded. Do NOT get together to film anything new!
  • Your video must be from a previous DERT main competition or Spotlight, or an equivalent 'performance' e.g. a festival spot or pub dance if you do not have a DERT video available.
  • The performance must be filmed from the front (or as near as possible) - no videos from the back of the dance please.
  • Try to choose a video where the whole dance is visible - feet and all!

You must send us your video by the 28th February 2021
#2
 Make sure that you're happy to send us the video

We're going to be displaying the videos on the tinter-webs so make sure that:

  • You're happy for us to do that. (If it's not already on the web)
  • Your team is in agreement.
  • You want to be judged by our official judges.
  • You want to be judged by everyone else. (Sorry not optional too many codes!)
#3
 Send us your video

Drop an email to "dertsteering@gmail.com" with the following:

  • The name of your team
  • Your name
  • Where the video is from
  • Which year the video is from
  • And then the video. Either a real video (no VHS) or a you tube or facebook link.
#4
 Wait For a bit and then join in

So here's the plan for Saturday:

  • We are going to upload your videos onto the DERT of DERTs part of DertInfo at the end of February.
  • Then over March the offical judges and going to use thier web skills, watch the videos and submit their scores and comments.
  • On the morning of Saturday the 27th March we are:
    • going to give you your scores and comments from the officals judges (and)
    • going to open up judging for all the submissions to everyone.
    • we're going to send your public submissions to you as soon as they come in through your logins.
    • but we are not going to expose the collated scores. So you'll not be able to see who's won but you'll be able to see what scores you got. (sneeky) Will it be enough?
  • Then later:
    • we'll publish the collated results from the offical judges so you can see who's won offically
    • we'll publish the collated results from the public judges so you can see who's won by the peoples choice
    • give you full rights to say that "we woz robbed" if the people like you best

The plan for Sunday is:

  • Publish some talks from the rapper community aficionados. (See the talks page)
  • Have a zoom meeting with team representatives and the steering group to discuss all things DERT

If you have any further questions send the steering group and email (same one as you'll be sending us the videos on!)
